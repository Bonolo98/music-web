AWARD-WINNING SOUTH AFRICAN R&B ARTIST

BIO

Bonolo Solomon, known by his stage name, is a South African Trap Soul artist hailing from Kuruman, Northern Cape. Born on April 6, 1998, he's now based in Johannesburg. His musical journey began in 2009, and by 2012, he recorded his first song, setting the stage for an impressive career. Bonolo Solomon draws inspiration from artists like August Alsina, Chris Brown, Eminem, Lil Wayne, and Akon. Notably, he was crowned "King of JTG District" at the Northern Cape Hip Hop Awards in 2019 after releasing his debut EP, "The Trailer EP," in 2018. In 2021, he received the "Best R&B Singer of the Year" award from the Northern Cape Awards for his song "On Reserve" from the LP "Bipolar," released in 2019. With a dynamic style that blends melodic trap beats, introspective lyrics, and hip hop flows, Bonolo Solomon has performed at prestigious events like the Northern Cape Heritage Festival. He's a self-taught musician, beatmaker, and producer who's dedicated to sharing his message through music.
