INTROSPECTIVE RNB ARTIST

BIO

An Introspective RnB Artist, with contemporary elements tackling subjects relating to love & intimacy, heartbreak & betrayal, and social insecurities.

Born and raised in Kuruman, Northern Cape, Bolomon's journey through music began with poetry, rap, then eventually RnB when his friend introduced him to August Alsina's music in 2014, with new found love for singing and roots in Hip-Hop, RnB with some Hip-Hop elements seemed to be a calling for him.

While creatively infusing melodic range of modern day RnB and occasional subtleties of TrapSoul instrumentation, Bonolo's music aims to help listeners make sense oof what they feel by reflecting inward and asking themselves deep rooted questions.

Career Milestones
